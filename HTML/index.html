6 Reasons To Attend

Come and learn what visionary organisations are doing to create thriving workplaces where people are more creative, healthy and productive.

Increase Productivity

Fact: The brain in a positive mode is 31% more productive than the brain in a negative or stressed mode (University of Wisconsin).

Enhance Performance

Fact: Employees who feel welcome to be themselves exhibit higher individual performance (Dan Cable, London Business School).

Improve Well-being

Fact: Mindful leaders have decreased burnout and increased creativity, attention and health (Ellen Langer, Harvard University).

Unleash Creativity

Fact: Happy employees have 31% higher productivity and their creativity is 3 times higher (Harvard Business Review).

Increase Profits

Fact: Companies in the “Best Companies To Work For” category over a 4-year period increased their profits by 315% (Aon Hewitt).

Reduce Costs

Fact: Reducing stress reduces absenteeism that is estimated to cost Australian businesses $6.4 billion a year (Medibank).

The Three Pillars Of Mindful Leadership

The mindful qualities creating greater performance, compassion and discernment to help individuals and organizations flourish.

Self-Awareness

Mindful leaders understand how to respond wisely to stress and learn the skills to allow more intelligent and creative choices to challenging situations.

Authentcity

Mindful leaders bring their whole selves to work. They don’t have to act like someone else. They “walk the talk” and people experience them as honest and real.

Compassion

Mindful leaders value high quality relationships. They learn skills to create a culture of kindness and creativity rather than a culture of stress.

The Forum Experience

In 2015, learn about the pioneering work being taking place at Google, Intel, Facebook, Starbucks, the World Economic Forum, the UK Government and Bhutan’s Gross National Happiness Centre.
